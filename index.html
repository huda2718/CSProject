Shaikh Ebrahim Center

Shaikh Ebrahim Center is located in Muharraq, the second biggest city in Bahrain after Manama. The central building features an expansive library with historical manuscripts and books. Additionally, there are several cultural "houses" representing different areas of Bahraini culture, including one for Nawakhtha (fishermen), a 1900s style school, and a traditional coffee shop. You can view a 360 degree tour of the center here.

Bahrain Bay

Bahrain Bay is located in the heart of Manama, the capitol, and has gorgous views of the city center. It features a walkway where many cultural and artistic events take place, and delicious food. The design, murals, and "vibe" of Bahrain Bay combines the modern with the traditional, attracting hundreds of visitors every day. Explore more onthe official website.
